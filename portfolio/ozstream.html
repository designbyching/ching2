Client Ozstream
Location Sydney, Australia
Year 2024
Work Brand Design System

Empowering Australians

Ozstream is a social enterprise dedicated to empowering Australia’s creative and performing arts communities. By connecting musicians, artists, venues, and audiences through innovative technology, Ozstream fosters a sustainable ecosystem for the creative industries. Their flagship platform, GigBridge, launching in February 2025, is a demand-driven marketplace that directly links musicians with live music venues, offering a no-commission, subscription-based model for seamless collaboration. With a mission to ensure artists earn a living wage and a vision to bridge talent with opportunity, Ozstream is reshaping the future of Australia’s creative culture. Visit gigbridge.com.au to learn more and join the journey.

Our Role

Design by Ching partnered with Ozstream to create a versatile and scalable brand design system that reflects their mission to empower Australia’s creative industries. Under Mike Ching’s direction, we crafted a cohesive identity featuring a bold logo, vibrant color palette, and tailored typography, designed to resonate with artists, venues, and audiences. Our work included promotional materials like posters, t-shirts, and water bottles, as well as a user-centric design system for the GigBridge platform, ensuring scalability for future initiatives.


Our collaborative approach ensured Ozstream’s vision was at the forefront, resulting in a brand that’s both innovative and authentic. The design system supports their event management app and lays the foundation for GigBridge’s launch in February 2025, connecting musicians and venues seamlessly. Visit gigbridge.com.au to explore Ozstream’s vision and join the creative movement.
