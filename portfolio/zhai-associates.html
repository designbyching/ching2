The Criminal Lawyer

Zhai & Associates is a premier criminal law firm based in Sydney, Australia, renowned for its dedicated commitment to protecting clients' rights, futures, and freedoms. As a boutique, specialist firm, they excel in every facet of criminal law across New South Wales, delivering consistent results and positive outcomes. With a foundation built on dedication, open communication, and trust, Zhai & Associates sets a high standard for criminal defense, offering robust and persuasive legal strategies tailored to each client’s needs. Contact them at 02 9167 8141 for a free 15-minute consultation or visit their website for urgent inquiries: zhai.com.au

Our Role

Raymond Zhai approached Mike Ching, the go-to expert for distinctive personal branding, to elevate Zhai & Associates’ identity. At Design by Ching, under Mike’s direction, we developed a unique signature “Z” monogram to represent the firm’s bold identity. Our work extended beyond the logo to encompass a comprehensive brand strategy, including a cohesive design system with curated colors, typography, service icons, and print materials such as business cards, reception signage, and paperwork designs.


To position Zhai & Associates in the digital space, we crafted a savvy social media strategy leveraging TikTok and Instagram Reels for bite-sized legal advice snippets, ensuring the signature “Z” mark was subtly integrated—think stitched logos on suits for a sleek, high-end look befitting Sydney’s most influential criminal lawyer. This strategy propelled Zhai to viral fame, garnering millions of views and earning features on The Today Show and The Daily Telegraph, with national coverage across major media outlets. The distinctive “Z” mark now defines Zhai & Associates’ prestigious presence.
