Work Brand Naming + Brand Identity Design + Brand Strategy

Empowering Fitness, Boldly Designed

FHIIT is a dynamic fitness brand based in Colombo, Sri Lanka, dedicated to transforming lives through high-intensity interval training (HIIT). Located at Warehouse 4, Economic Center, Kirimandala Mawatha, Colombo 05 (Narahenpita), FHIIT offers a range of services including workouts, pre/postnatal fitness, corporate programs, and franchise opportunities. With a motto of “One Day or Day One... You Decide,” FHIIT inspires individuals to take charge of their fitness journey. Their vibrant community and accessible approach make fitness approachable and exciting. Visit fhiit.lk to book a free session and explore their offerings.

Our Role

Design by Ching collaborated with FHIIT to create a bold and memorable brand identity that captures the essence of high-intensity interval training. Our work began with crafting the brand name “FHIIT.lk,” a dynamic and catchy name that reflects functional fitness and intensity. We then developed a comprehensive Brand Identity Design, including a neurodesign-inspired logo, vibrant color palette (yellow and dark charcoal/black), and approachable typography that aligns with FHIIT’s energetic persona. Finally, we formulated a Brand Strategy to ensure consistency and alignment with FHIIT’s mission to make fitness accessible and impactful.


The FHIIT logo leverages neurodesign principles for maximum impact. The “F” represents “Functional” fitness, with accelerating bars symbolizing the increasing intensity of workouts. The curvy, tapered font is approachable, conveying that fitness progress is achievable. The inclined font aligns with left-to-right reading patterns, suggesting motion toward goals. Using the Gestalt principle of proximity, the logo’s horizontal bars form a single “F,” enhancing memorability. The vibrant yellow color adds a “kick,” evoking happiness and enthusiasm, while dark charcoal/black conveys strength and sophistication. The “HIIT” portion uses thick, straight lines to symbolize structure, strength, and the intervals of high-intensity training. Visit fhiit.lk to explore FHIIT’s bold brand and fitness offerings.
